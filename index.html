About DoorChitraVani

Welcome to DoorChitraVani, an open-source static app designed for fun, study, research, and learning. This app is completely free to use and doesn't intend any copyright or violation. We prioritize user privacy and comply with GDPR regulations, ensuring that no user data is sent or stored on our servers.

DoorChitraVani provides a versatile interface to play various media formats, including HLS, audio, video, MP3, MP4, OGG, display images, and open PDFs. However, please note that this app doesn't host or provide any content itself. It's up to the users to ensure they have the necessary permissions or rights to access the content they use within the app.

By using DoorChitraVani, you acknowledge that the developer is not responsible for any misuse or potential issues that may arise. We're committed to providing a useful tool while respecting user privacy and adhering to applicable laws.
